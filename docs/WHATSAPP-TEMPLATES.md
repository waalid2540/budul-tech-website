## Tips for WhatsApp Communication

1. **Respond within 2 hours** during business hours
2. **Use emojis** - they increase engagement
3. **Keep it short** - WhatsApp is for quick messages
4. **Be personal** - use their name, reference their business
5. **Send voice notes** for complex explanations
6. **Share images** of content/results when possible
